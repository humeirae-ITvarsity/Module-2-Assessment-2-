Ocean Basket is South Africa's favourite seafood restaurant, committed to giving you delicious seafood and that 'home-away-from-home' feeling to remind you of what it's like to sit at the family table and share a meal with your nearest and dearest.
We offer an array of Mediterranean inspired dishes from prawns, sushi, and calamari to our famous fish and chips and, of course, our legendary creamy lemon sauce. Visit any of our restaurants to share one of our bountiful platters, dip your prawn into our trio of sauces or dig into our complimentary bread.
